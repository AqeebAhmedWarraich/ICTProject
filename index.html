Sports at GIK Institute

The sports complex at GIKI is equipped with modern infrastructure and facilities catering to a wide range of sports, including cricket, football, basketball, tennis, squash, badminton and table tennis. It provides students with the opportunity to engage in both competitive and leisurely sports, fostering a healthy and active lifestyle among the GIKI community. Key features of the GIKI Sports Complex include: 1: Multiple Sports Arenas 2: Fitness Facilities 3: Organized Events 4: Recreational Spaces
