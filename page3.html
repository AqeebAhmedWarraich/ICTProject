Football At GIK

GIKI Football Team

As ambassadors of GIKI's sporting culture, the football team continues to uphold the institute's values through their dedication, skill, and sportsmanship on and off the field, leaving an indelible mark on the institute's sporting history. They stand as a symbol of pride, representing GIKI with distinction and contributing significantly to the vibrant sports legacy of the institution. The GIKI football team comprises dedicated and skilled players who showcase their prowess on the field through their teamwork, technique, and commitment to the sport. They participate in inter-university competitions, local tournaments, and often engage in friendly matches with other institutions, showcasing their abilities and representing GIKI with pride.
